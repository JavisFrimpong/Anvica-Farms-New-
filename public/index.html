Our Story

Welcome to Anvica Farms

Located in Konongo, Ashanti Region, Anvica Farms is a trusted name in premium poultry farming in Ghana. We are dedicated to raising healthy, happy birds using sustainable farming practices that produce the freshest, most nutritious poultry products for your family.

From our carefully managed free-range environments to our strict hygiene standards, every step is taken to ensure you receive only the best. We believe in quality over quantity, delivering farm-fresh products that you can trust.
